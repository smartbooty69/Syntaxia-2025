BGMI GAMING

Upgrade, loot, conquer! Syntaxia's Gameplan: The ultimate mobile Battle Royale. Ditch textbooks, be the last squad standing. Skill, strategy, luck - dominate or be a cautionary tale. Go forth and conquer!

Event Heads: Yashwanth, Manu David


Rules and Regulations

  1. Max 4 teams per college.
  2. 4 squad teams.
  3. This is a full day event.
  4. Cheating hacks or emulators are strictly prohibited. If any squad is found using them, they will be immediately disqualified.
  5. GFX tools and 3rd party apps like game boosters are prohibited.
  6. Any form of cheating or usage of mods will lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. Participants must bring their own mobiles.
  8. Participants will have to use their own data packs.
  9. Each team must consist of 4 players. If the team has less than 4-players then contact the Event Heads.
